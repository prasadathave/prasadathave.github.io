About Us

IR&P is the student counil mentored by IIT Gandhinagar faculties. IR&P has been mainly focusing on building the industry-academia retions. Council has been conducting various events and talks for creating student awareness.

What we do?

IR&P firmly believes that no student can become a good engineer until he/she has practiced enough of the real life industry problems. As industrial problems have their own requirement of skillset which can help student who solves them to improve his/her personal technical field related skills and solving these problems can help both industry and academia. With this vision IR&P does the following things -



IR&P has brought projects from many companies like TATA Chemicals, Honeywell etc. for students to work upon them as a project course.

IR&P has helped to solve the institute level problems by floating the projects into student community under EWYL policy of IIT GN.

IR&P has acted as a platform for organising various hackathons,talks and workshops. Council has been instrumental in providing a platform for the development of entrepreneurial activities.

Council has been spreading awareness about the national and international academic competitions and council has also provided students the required logistic support for participating in this.
